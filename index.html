About Dr. Neeraj Gupta

Dr. Neeraj Gupta is a renowned holistic coach and mental wellness expert with over a decade of experience in guiding individuals toward emotional freedom and inner peace. His integrative approach combines mindfulness, cognitive reframing, and compassionate healing to address anxiety, stress, and deep-seated emotional blocks.

He has helped hundreds of clients across India — from Jammu to Mumbai — transform their lives. His mission is to make lasting mental well-being accessible to all, and his coaching style is warm, non-judgmental, and results-oriented.

Dr. Neeraj holds advanced certifications in counseling psychology and mind-body medicine. He regularly conducts workshops and one-on-one sessions, empowering people to reconnect with their authentic selves.

Core Values

  • Compassion — Every session is held in a safe, empathetic space.
  • Authenticity — Honest guidance rooted in real transformation.
  • Holistic Growth — Addressing mind, body, and spirit for complete healing.

Counselling Approach

My coaching methodology is personalized, evidence-informed, and deeply human. I blend modern psychology with ancient wisdom to help you break free from limiting patterns. Each session is tailored to your unique needs, whether you're dealing with anxiety, emotional trauma, or simply seeking more clarity and purpose.

What to Expect

  • Initial Discovery Call — A no-pressure conversation to understand your challenges and goals.
  • Personalized Roadmap — Together we design a step-by-step plan for your healing journey.
  • Ongoing Support — Regular video sessions, practical exercises, and accountability.
  • Long-Term Resilience — Tools and insights that stay with you for life.

All sessions are conducted online, so you can receive world-class coaching from the comfort of your home. I work with clients across India and internationally.
